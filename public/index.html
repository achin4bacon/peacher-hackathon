Welcome to Peacher for parents and teachers! Peacher is an interactive program designed to maintain communication between parents and teachers. For parents - you can check grades, make sure your child is keeping up with assignments, and contact your child's teachers. For teachers - Peacher gives you the ability to submit grades, post assignments, and check class averages.
